# xtpcmg 1.0.0

* Initial CRAN release.
* Group-Mean FM-OLS following Wagner and Reichold (2023).
* Pooled FM-OLS following de Jong and Wagner (2022).
* HAC long-run variance estimation with Andrews (1991) automatic bandwidth.
* Turning point analysis with delta-method confidence intervals.
* Swamy (1970) slope homogeneity test.
* Polynomial degree 2 (quadratic EKC) and 3 (cubic) supported.
